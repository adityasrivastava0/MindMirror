🌿 Mental Health Resources

✨ Self-Care Strategies

  • Mindfulness: Practice 5-10 minutes of meditation daily to calm your mind.
  • Sleep Hygiene: Go to bed and wake up at the same time every day.
  • Physical Activity: Try light exercise like walking or yoga for 30 minutes.
  • Social Connection: Talk to a friend or loved one regularly.
  • Journaling: Express your emotions and thoughts on paper daily.

🚨 Crisis Support

If you're in immediate crisis:

Call your local emergency number or visit the nearest hospital.

📍 United States

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988

Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
