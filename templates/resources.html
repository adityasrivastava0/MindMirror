Mental Health Resources

Self-Care Strategies

  • Mindfulness: Practice 5-10 minutes of meditation daily
  • Sleep Hygiene: Maintain consistent sleep schedule
  • Physical Activity: 30 minutes of movement daily
  • Social Connection: Reach out to one person daily
  • Journaling: Write about your thoughts and feelings

Crisis Support

If you're in immediate crisis:

Call your local emergency number or go to the nearest hospital

United States:

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988

Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

International:

Find your local crisis line at IASP
